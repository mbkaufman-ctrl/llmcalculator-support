Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

The short version: LLM Calculator does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully respected.

Data Collection

LLM Calculator does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. The app operates entirely on your device.

Network Access

The app may access the internet solely to retrieve publicly available model information from Hugging Face. No personal data is transmitted during these requests.

Third-Party Services

LLM Calculator does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs collecting data in the background.

Data Storage

Any preferences or settings you configure are stored locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.

Support

Having trouble with LLM Calculator? Here are some common things to check:

Model data isn't loading

Make sure you have an active internet connection. The app fetches model information from Hugging Face's public API. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

RAM calculation seems off

The calculator estimates RAM usage based on model parameters and quantization format. Actual usage may vary slightly depending on your system's available memory and other running applications.

About

LLM Calculator was built to solve a simple problem: figuring out which AI models can actually run on your hardware without downloading them first.

It calculates RAM requirements across different quantization formats and context window sizes, giving you a clear compatibility rating in seconds. Browse thousands of GGUF models from Hugging Face and make informed decisions about local AI.
